Magalwewa Hideout This is a 7 bedroom villa and recreational facilities such as swimming pool, sauna and camping- hiking activities. The building, designed to obtain the maximum view of the magnificent and man made ancient lake call “Magal Wewa” which located in Hambantota District in Southern Province, Sri Lanka. Except the view of the lake other major task is to reduce the construction time of the building, therefore we decided to use shipping container boxes as a major building material. The construction plan is, while steel structure work continues at the site, planning to do all interior work outside of the site where the workshop is situated and fabricate all shipping container works and transport it to the site when the structure is ready. We started all schematic design work by planning all construction mythology of the container fabrications.
Location Suriyawewa, Sri Lanka
Involvement Architectural design and development
Client Mr. Lapitha Gunasighe
Status Design development
Solution / Concept Design to facilitate fast construction method and moduler fabrication system.
