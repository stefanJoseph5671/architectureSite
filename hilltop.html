Hilltop House When the initial concept formulation we observe the land and also client of the house to identify personal lifestyle. House design should personalize to its own occupants. It is imperative that the house is personalized, unique, and specialized exclusively for the particular family that lives there. In this particular house design, we tried to obtain beautiful views of the surrounding of the house while rotation the space to different angles. Since the land is situated top of the small hill it gives nice sceneries. Ground floor is for all public activities such as living, dining, kitchen, etc. First floor is for 3 bedrooms including a master bedroom with attached toilet. The second floor is of the studio room and open terrace to enjoy the nice views.
Location : Kahawatta, Sri Lanka
Client : Mr. Dushmantha Baranage
Land Extent : 156 Perches
Building Area : 4000 Sqft
Involvement : Architectural design and development/ Project Management
Principle Architect : K. A. Daham Thisula
Contractor : Local team
Status : Under Construction
