Budget House The initial idea was to design the house that responds to the minimum requirements of the client to minimize the cost of the construction by reducing building footprint and create cozy interior spaces. This is a single bedroom house situated at the Raigama village Sri Lanka.
Location : Raigama, Sri Lanka
Client : Mr. Janaka Kuruppu
Land Extent : 40 Perches
Building Area : 1500 Sqft
Involvement : Architectural/ Interior design and development / Project management
Principle Architect : K. A. Daham Thisula
Status : Completed in 2015
