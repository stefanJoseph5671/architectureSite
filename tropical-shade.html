The Tropical Shade Four bedroom house was designed to cater five member family who is living a busy lifestyle in the capital. Basic idea was to create greener and homy spaces inside to relax with their family members. Client wanted the traditional shady environment but not the vernacular style house. Therefor, we design a house is very modern tropical with the traditional interior spaces by introducing open courtyards and natural light and ventilation.
Location : Battaramulla, Sri Lanka
Client : Dr. Aravinda Disanayake
Land Extent : 24 Perches
Building Area : 4500 Sqft
Involvement : Architectural design and development / Project management
Principle Architect : K. A. Daham Thisula
Structural Engineer: Dulan Sendanayake
Quantity Surveyor : Bhagya Nadun
Contractor : Niwasa Constructions Pvt, Ltd
Status : Under construction
